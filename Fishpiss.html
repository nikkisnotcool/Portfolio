Fish Piss IPA

The packaging of this IPA is inspired by classic British and pub decor, with fun typography and corner ornaments. The happy fish supports a gentlemanly tophat, contrasting with the subject matter. The orgin of this concept is that this is an isinglass free beer. Isinglass is a traditional filter for British-style beers made from fishs' swim bladders. So, this British-style IPA proudly teases this fact. The box is designed to mimic watery waves.
