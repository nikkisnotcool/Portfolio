Colibrí Peruvian Fusion Menu

Inspired by my love of Peruvian food and coffee, I created branding and a menu for this fictional restaurant Colibrí. The brand look of this is is to combine the ancient motifs of peru with modern design aesthetics to call attention to the fusion of foods from different worlds and times. The humingbird in the logo is a fusion of modern hummingbird iconography and the way birds are depicted in Peruvian textiles. The menu section dividers are also inspired by the these textiles.
