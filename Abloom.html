Abloom App

Abloom is the app that allows you to find plants based on your enviroment. The app uses many specific search filters from sun exposure to animal resistance to help users find plants that will thrive in their area. Users can save search filter presets to "my climates" for easy searching. The "my favourites" function allows the bookmarking of specific plants for later review. An engaging part of this app is the interactivity of the flowers. When tapped, they will sway back and forth.

I created Abloom for my thesis because of my experience working in a greenhouse. While working there, it is very clear that the enviroment and needs of the people end up deciding the selection of plants that can exist there. Abloom helps solve this problem by allowing users to input that information, and save plants they wish to keep in mind.
