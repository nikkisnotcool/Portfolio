Pals & Paws

Pals & Paws is a local business that specializes in products for dogs, cats, and small animals, while also providing amazing grooming services! This business focuses on local and organic products. They also provide high quality grooming services from very experienced groomers for kittens, cats, dogs and puppies. It's possible they'll also expand to dog training services in the future as well.

The handmade type of the logo reflects the how we think of our pets as family and our best friends, fun and comfortable. The doggy face immediately identifies the business as being one for pet owners. A bright blue colour helps communicate friendliness, knowledge, modern thinking, and optimism. The green accent colours are used to portray the natural, local side of the business, and together with the blue create an outdoorsy palette of land, lakes and sky. The characters Kit and Pup brighten up the design materials with their little smiles, bringing that happy, friendly feeling to the rest of the branding.
