Community Options

This design project was done during a great chairity event known as Creative Day For Social Good (CD4SG). Teams made up of students, an art director and an account manager work within a 24 hour period to provide design materials to a non-profit organization.

Nicole Steinberg, Connor Quenneville and myself worked with our art director Chris Walton and account manager Breanna Lalonde to produce great work for Linden Colette, executive director of Community Options for Children and Families in British Columbia.

The Client required a new identiy system along with brochure and social media templates. We chose red heart to represent the participants great hearts, the support of their families, and love that goes into volunteering at Community Options. This red also pairs well with the teal they had been using as their colour for over 30 years. Text materials were intentionally made high contrast and with larger text to allow those with visual imparement to look over the information easier.
