Hobbyist Aquarium Magazine

This magazine was designed with the dedicated and diverse group of people who enjoy fishkeeping in mind. People in the hobby are always learning and using tips and tricks to improve and lower costs for their tanks. The design of this magazine combines artistic and mechanical, just like fishkeeping. Fish owners balance water chemistry and tank machinery with beautiful aquascapes and amazing creatures. This is also why taking stunning, vibrant photographs was important to this project.

Logo design, layout design, illustration and photography are all my own.
